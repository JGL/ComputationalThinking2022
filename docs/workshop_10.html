For this workshop we are going to spend 30 minutes each on making p5.js sketches that include the following libraries:
  1. p5.sound
  2. Rita.js
  3. p5.gui
For the first 30 minutes, let's take a look at the p5.js reference page for the p5.sound library, and some of the examples there.
Now, let's take a look at the examples included on ditor.p5.org - how to search the examples and particularly the index.html code to include the p5.sound.js file.
For the next 30 minutes, let's take a look at rita.js by Daniel Howe. Let's have a look at the examples on the p5.js online editor and also some of the projects that have been made with it.
Helpfully, Allison Parrish has posted a great tutorial on how to use external JavaScript libraries with p5.js (with rita.js as the example library), let's go through it together and get it going in the p5.js editor.
For the final 30 minutes, let's put everything together by duplicating some of the p5.gui examples from GitHub, adding them to a new editor.p5js sketch, and coming up with some new things to do with our GUI.
Thanks! Time for a short break!
